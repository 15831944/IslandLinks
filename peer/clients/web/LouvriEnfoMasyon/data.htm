Welcome to the latest Island Software community experiment. This time the goal is to help build a Knowledge Base by asking questions. Although we are obviously still working on this page, the first step is to ask lots of questions so that we continually interact with the KB to get a baseline of coverage. The second step after asking the question is to "tag" the entry to give it as much context as possible. On social media, blogs, etc tag your entry with #LouvriEnfomasyon+ and our "Krawler" will eventually find it (at least on most major social sites). We will eventually have a place to enter urls to be "Krawled" here as well. Just go crazy with the tagging, the more context the better. Questions aren't the only thing you can tag, but try to use the categories we have now. We can distribute the post (when we find it) and enter it into the KB for later use. An example would be a post that has "Why does this metal rust? #LouvriEnfomasyon+ #Questions". Eventually we should be able to automate the answer to questions and problems or present our "best guess" become more and more accurate over time. To all involved, watch this space, and thanks for your help!
-The Administrator
